Overview

Wek Shalf is a casual mobile game intended for a general audience and suitable for ages 7+. We do not directly collect personally identifiable information. Some third‑party services we use (listed below) may process device and app data under their own privacy policies to provide ads, purchases, analytics, diagnostics, and performance monitoring.

Data We Process

  • Local game data: settings (e.g., sound, language) and progress, stored on the device.
  • Network data: IP address, device/OS information, app version, advertising identifiers (if available), used by third parties for delivery, analytics, and fraud prevention.

Third‑Party Services

  • Advertising — Google AdMob: delivers age‑appropriate ads. We do not allow adult or gambling content. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. More: Google Privacy Policy · AdMob Policy.
  • In‑app Purchases — Google Play Billing: purchases are processed by Google. We do not receive or store payment card details.
  • Analytics — Firebase Analytics: event metrics to understand gameplay and improve features. We use aggregated reports only.
  • Diagnostics — Firebase Crashlytics: crash logs and stack traces to fix issues. Logs may include device/OS info and anonymized identifiers.
  • Performance Monitoring — Firebase Performance: network and rendering performance metrics to improve stability.

Children

The game is suitable for ages 7+. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child provided us with personally identifiable information, contact us and we will remove it.

Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with the service providers above or when required by law.

Retention and Deletion

Local data remains on your device while the app is installed. You can delete it by uninstalling the app or using in‑game reset options if available. Aggregated analytics and diagnostics are retained by providers under their policies.

Your Choices

  • Limit personalized ads via your device/Google Account settings.
  • Disable analytics/diagnostics from your OS privacy settings where supported.

Permissions

The app requires Internet access for ads, purchases, and updates. It does not request sensitive permissions such as camera or precise location unless a feature explicitly needs them.

Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above reflects the latest version.
